# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
## Government Auditing System Mexico

### OBJECTIVE

To create an automated system to download, validate, and certify official audit formats from the Mexican government with **maximum legal validity** before the Supreme Court of Justice of the Nation (SCJN).

### SCOPE

- **Source**: Secretariat of Anti-Corruption and Good Government
- **Documents**: {num_hashes} official audit formats
- **Fiscal year**: 2021
- **Coverage**: Audits of states and financial information

### METHODOLOGY

1. **Automated Download**: Python scripts for verifiable download
2. **Cryptographic Validation**: SHA-256 hash of each document
3. **Blockchain Anchoring**: OpenTimestamps on the Bitcoin blockchain
4. **NOM-151 Certification**: Conservation certificate for data messages
5. **Chain of Custody**: Complete record of all events
6. **Drive Sync**: Backup on Google Drive

### LEGAL VALIDITY

The system complies with:

#### NOM-151-SCFI-2016
Official Mexican Standard for the conservation of data messages:
- ✓ Identification of data messages
- ✓ Cryptographic hashes
- ✓ Timestamps
- ✓ Chain of custody
- ✓ Integrity guarantee

#### Commerce Code
Article 89 bis: Data messages have the same validity as physical documents.

#### SCJN
Thesis 2026752: Electronic documents have full evidentiary value when their authenticity and integrity are proven.

#### Bitcoin Blockchain
Immutable proof of existence through anchoring on a public and decentralized blockchain.
